Generate Assessments Tool

This application allows you to efficiently generate assessment forms using the provided Excel (.xlsx) format. Blank columns will not have assessments generated.

Once you've filled out your assessments, click on the "Choose Files" button to select your files, and then hit the "Upload" button to process them.

Please note that the excel template provides simplified descriptions to assist with easy form generation. For further clarification, refer to form downloadable below:

  • WHODAS
  • WHODAS children and youth version
  • CANS
  • LSP
  • Lawton-Brody IADL
  • LEFS
  • Berg Balance Scale
  • FRAT
  • HONOS
  • CASP
